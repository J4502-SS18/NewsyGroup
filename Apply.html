Apply for the Newsy Student Intern Program

To be involved in Newsy's independent study, student will work two four-hour shifts each week in Newsy's Columbia newsroom throughout the semester. They will be tasked with pitching and writing stories as well as editing videos as part of a professor-led student production team.

Interested students must enroll in either JOURN 4050 or JOURN 4350.

Any students with further questions should feel free to contact Nathan Byrne at byrne@newsy.com
